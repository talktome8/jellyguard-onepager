## ğŸ’¼ Legal & Ethical Notes

### Why Honesty Matters
1. **Regulatory compliance**: False claims can lead to legal issues
2. **Investor relations**: Misrepresentation harms fundraising
3. **Client trust**: One discovered lie destroys credibility
4. **Long-term reputation**: Truth builds sustainable business

### What's Allowed
- **Aspirational language**: "Designed to...", "Aims to..."
- **Conditional claims**: "Lab testing suggests...", "Research indicates..."
- **Feature descriptions**: Describe what it IS, not what it WILL DO (until proven)

### What's Not Allowed
- **Fabricated testimonials**: Always illegal
- **False statistics**: Can be fraud
- **Misleading comparisons**: Without data
- **Guaranteed outcomes**: Without proof
